Just before you climb to the top you smell something rotten. Carefully you peak over the edge. You see a dead Formian worker lying on the ground. On closer examination it turns out that the body is sucked dry of blood and left to rot in the sun. Evil has a firmer grip in this area (reroll taints)

Walking on either side of the water you are suddenly confronted by a Myrmarch called Hosan. He acts a bit creepy. If party does not cooperate he will force them to do his work or die trying.

  • How they got here?
  • What are the adventurers up to?
  • Do you know what "the light" did to our Queen?
  • He can heal some wounds.
  • He can fix the boat.
  • He wants a task master dead.
  • Why? That taskmaster is under control of Fihni and he wants another taskmaster close to the light. One that he controls.
  • His advice is to go in during the day and circumvent the patrols or enter during the night through level-1.
