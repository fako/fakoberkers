There are two different ways of approaching the Pink Flower Tree.

  • Following the stream the party gets a SpotDC15 to notice the tree before effects take place.
  • When coming from the jungle they suddenly behold the magnificent tree and it is too late (see traps).
{% endblock %} {% block locations %}

As you enter the area you see a huge ancient tree under the waterfall. Its roots curl around the rocks and go into the small pond. On the roots on the rocks grow beautiful big flowers. The flowers are completely open and are inviting to sit down in for a short break.

From here there are a few places to go.

  • Go up the 200m high waterfall (4xClimbDC 15/20) to top
  • Follow to waterfall stream to the left
  • Go into the jungle across the tree
  • Go into the jungle on the right side
