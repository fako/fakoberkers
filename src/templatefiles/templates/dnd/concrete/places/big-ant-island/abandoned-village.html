There are various points of interest in this large area with relatively little vegetation. It is striking that there are no signs of

  • A stream of water that ends in the sea.
  • A few stone houses with their wooden roofs collapsed and walls with huge tears stand in a circle.
  • On the edge of the small village lies a boat with a whole in its bough.
  • In the center of the circle of houses is an old fire place.

  • Go back to the beach
  • Move into the jungle
  • Up the headland to your left. This seems a very though climb (ClimbDC25) due to steepness (+5) and the thick growth (+5).
