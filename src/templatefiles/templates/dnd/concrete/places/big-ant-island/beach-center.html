  • You're in a bay in the shape of a moon.
  • Beyond the bay the sea stretches as far as you can see.
  • The "arms" of the bay are steep rocks that lead up to an even steeper landscape in the middle.
  • What lies beyond that huge rock you do not know.
  • From the beach you can get to the gargantuan rock by going through a jungle landscape.
