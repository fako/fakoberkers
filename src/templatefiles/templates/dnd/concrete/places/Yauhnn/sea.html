When entering Yauhnn through the sea for the first time the adventurers have a few options depending on their situation:

  • When they were helped Sjahnsesh will lead them to fishers boats (evade guards)
  • Go to bright harbor with their condition.
  • Try to go for darker area (which are breakers slums)
{% endblock %} {% block locations %}

From the sea Yauhnn looks very impressive. There are boats everywhere. The city consists of tiers. The further up you go the richer the houses look. One area very near the docks also looks expensive though.

When on the sea around Yauhnn. There are a few ways to get to mainland

  • The breaker slums is toughest area to get to land and will lead to stranding.
  • The harbor is easiest, but small boats are not allowed.
  • The fisher boats is easiest and safest way to get to land. Second tier of city is guarded.

There is also a secret way to on land. When defying the currents somebody can enter a submarine cave that leads to the entrance of a secret route in sea water slums.
