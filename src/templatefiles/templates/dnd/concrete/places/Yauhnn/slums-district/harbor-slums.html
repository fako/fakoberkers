A very poor part of the city. The houses were once constructed fairly well, but due to the lack of maintenance the houses look shabby. Trash from unloading ships lies around everywhere. There is also a faint smell of rotting fish.

  • Walk towards the sea for fisher boats
  • Against the cliff face there is a small prison

A lot of people live here. A little less cramped than in the caves, but still small quarters and poor. An inn called The Smiling Fish is the only noticeable place here.
