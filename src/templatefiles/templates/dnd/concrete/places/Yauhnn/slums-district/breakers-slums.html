{% endblock %} {% block locations %}

The houses here are nothing but a few wooden planks holding some fleece as a roof. Most houses do not even have a door. It would probably smell terrible if the wind wasn't blowing so hard around here.

Landmarks

  • In the middle of the area there is a big pool. Children are playing there during the day (see events)
  • There is one shack next to big pool where children are constantly crying. Inside there are children with wounded hands.

Neighbooring area's

  • There is a stair winding up the cliff to Lighthouse / The Church of Valkur
  • The cliff face is crawling with small caves forming The Cave Slums
  • The pool in the middle of this area leads to The Sea Water Slums (SpotDC 13: to notice swimmers deeper in the pool)
  • Breakers slums is closest to the open sea (which brings some hazards)
  • You can go to area where fisher boats are docked

View

  • The docks
  • Massive building with a huge round window showing a face enprofile in glass and lead (see ReligionDC)
  • Walls with a tower on sea side and some kind of fortress in the middle of the bay
  • Behind the ships the whole bank consists of noble houses. The ones closer to the water are smaller.
