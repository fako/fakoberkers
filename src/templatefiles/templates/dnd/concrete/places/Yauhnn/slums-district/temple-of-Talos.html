First time

When entering for the first time (through temple of Valkur)

  1. Adventures enter through curtains to a bigger cave.
  2. Two half-Locathah are in a bloody fist fight right in front of their noses.
  3. Many Locathah and half Locathah are watching the fight
  4. A female cleric also stands on the altar and recognizes the adventurers as figures from the prophecy
  5. Everybody drops to their knees in worship
  6. All wounds and taint is taken care off by Janala

Second time

When entering second time Janala explains her gifts. Then offers a demonstration and does a definition. "where ever the faithful suffer, Talos his work must be done"

{% endblock %} {% block locations %}

This big cave is full of decorations hinting at Talos. On one side there is a slight elevation in the rocks. This is used as an altar to the God of Destruction.

From here adventurers can go to:

  • The stairs behind the curtains on the alter leading to temple of Valkur
  • A small pathway in the back of the cave leads to slums market
