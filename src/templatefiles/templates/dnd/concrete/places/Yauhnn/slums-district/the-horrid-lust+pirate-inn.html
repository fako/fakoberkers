When party defeats the group the dread pirate is impressed and asks if they'd be interested in a job.

{% endblock %} {% block locations %}

The ship looks neglected. Sea weed has grown up to the bough. It sails have started to mould. You wonder when it will sink.

The deck is a big mess of old stuff. From the inside the boat looks a lot better. It is clean and well preserved. A stairs leads to one big room where the cargo used to be. It is turned into a rather luxurious inn.
