{% extends "dnd/abstract/place.html" %} {% block title %}The fisher boats{% endblock %} {% block summary %}

Safest place to get to land for smaller boats without paying harbor fees. Bit of a market place for fishermen, but only unofficially.

{% endblock %} {% block events %}

At night guards are walking the second tier of the city with torches. They overlook the fisher boats, but are not terribly enthusiastic to go down.

As adventurers walk by during the day, Sjahnsesh makes a remark about shabby equipment. He suggests to take them to some good (shady) employers at The Horrid Lust.

{% endblock %} {% block locations %}

Nobody is living in this area. There are just a lot of boats and fishing nets everywhere. It smells like rotten fish.

View

  • Massive building with a huge round window showing a face enprofile in glass and lead (see ReligionDC)
  • Walls with a tower on sea side and some kind of fortress in the middle of the bay
  • Behind the ships the whole bank consists of noble houses. The ones closer to the water are smaller.
