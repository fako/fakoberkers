Walking around you see many sheds setup in the cave complex. Many half-Locathah and some poor humans live here packed on each other. Especially deeper into the caves it is filthy and impossible to see without light. More towards the cliffs there is a wind coming from the sea, which makes it more bearable. These sheds also have a stunning view.

From here the adventurers can go to:

  • Climb the stairs up the cliff side to temple of Valkur
  • Work your way down the caves and through narrow alley to slums market (FortDC12: nauseated, see BreathDC)
  • Climb down the stairs on the cliff side to breakers slums
