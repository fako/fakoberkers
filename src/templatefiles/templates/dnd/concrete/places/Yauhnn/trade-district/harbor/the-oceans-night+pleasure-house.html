About the girl

Her name is Rafana Riebelfant sister of Olivet. She loves animals and plants. She only recently moved to her brother's place in Yauhnn and especially enjoys the nightlife of the city. Dancing excites her most, but she also likes the thrill of gambling, especially if it is not her money.

About the man

His name is Taduan Kaler. He runs a few businesses in animal life in Yauhnn and since a few years he has been elected as Hulorn of the city. He's especially interested in your opinions about trade and politics and their relation. He thinks that policy should never stand in the way of trade.

Gambling:

Gambling can go well and suddenly very bad. On average during an hour of play there will be a loss or a profit X. This depends highly on the stakes Y. Although luck always play a big part some games allow for more tactics expressed with a value Z between 0 and 1. A game has Z = (1d10 - 4) / 10.

X = Y * (1d20 + (Int*Z) - 11)
