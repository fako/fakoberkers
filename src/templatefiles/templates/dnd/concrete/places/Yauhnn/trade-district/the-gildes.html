This area is filled mostly with well guarded warehouses. Some of them have a shop. You see mostly clothing, fabrics and wood shops. The shops do not look very wealthy, but seem well maintained.

Apart from the warehouses this area harbors most guilds:

  • Wood workers guild
  • Carpenter guild
  • Smiths guild
  • Miners guild

Some other venues:

  • There is an inn where lots of artisans and merchants come together. Elves pay double there, because there are quite a few lumberjacks with bad experiences.
  • Illegal gamble house

From here adventurers can:

  • Go to big market
  • Go up the ramp to North side nobles
  • Go down the ramp to wannabe nobles
  • Go into the mining entrance
