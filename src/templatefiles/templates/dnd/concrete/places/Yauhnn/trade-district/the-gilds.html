On the other side of the big market than the city center and less "touristic". It is a residential area, but also has some influential gilds.

Apart from the warehouses this area harbors most gilds:

  • Traders gild
  • Wood workers gild
  • Carpenter gild
  • Smiths gild
  • Miners gild

Some other venues:

  • There is an inn where lots of artisans and merchants come together. Elves pay double there, because there are quite a few lumberjacks with bad experiences.
  • Illegal gamble house

From here adventurers can:

  • Go to big market
  • Or the other way to the goods
  • Go up the ramp to North side nobles
  • Go down the ramp to the dock nobles
  • Or down the ramp to the harbor
  • Go into the mining entrance
