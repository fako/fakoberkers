{% extends "dnd/abstract/place.html" %} {% block title %}The Temple of Waukeen{% endblock %} {% block summary %}A place of worship for merchant. Main entrance to the market.{% endblock %} {% block events %}

At night there are guards at the top of the stairs who check people walking up.

Walking up the stairs an assassination attempt takes place.

{% endblock %} {% block locations %}

The entrance

  • At the base of the temple is the weighing square where goods wait for weighing.
  • Two big scales on each side of the stairs lead to the next city level.
  • An elevator in right "pillar", leads to next city level and 1st + 2nd floor
  • Stairs to all 3 levels in the left pillar, usually guarded

Neighbouring area's

  • That weighing square on city ground level is the entry to the harbor
  • On the north side the weighing square leads to harbor slums
  • On the south sides weighing square leads to dock side nobles
  • The temple lies in front of and is entry to the big market on 1st city level

Inside

  • 1st floor, sacred shrine, where goods are blessed inside the elevator/scale
  • 2nd floor, storage where confiscated/traded goods are kept
  • 3rd floor, more (precious) storage and clerics/monks sleeping quarters
