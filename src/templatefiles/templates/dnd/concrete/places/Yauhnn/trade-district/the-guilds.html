On the other side of the big market than the city center and less "touristic". It is a residential area, but also has some influential guilds.

Apart from the warehouses this area harbors most guilds:

  • Traders guild (stock exchange)
  • Wood workers guild
  • Carpenter guild
  • Smiths guild
  • Miners guild

Some other venues:

  • There is an inn where lots of artisans and merchants come together. Elves pay double there, because there are quite a few lumberjacks with bad experiences. Cormyr citizens are also frowned upon for their taxing system.
  • Illegal gamble house

From here adventurers can:

  • Go to big market
  • Or the other way to the goods
  • Go up the ramp to North side nobles
  • Go down the ramp to the dock nobles
  • Or down the ramp to the harbor
  • Go into the mining entrance
