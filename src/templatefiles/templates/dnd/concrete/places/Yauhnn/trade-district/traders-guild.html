{% extends "dnd/abstract/place.html" %} {% block title %}Traders Guild{% endblock %} {% block summary %}A luxurious guild for all merchants. Stock is "exchanged" here.{% endblock %} {% block todo %}Make selling pitch for Falok.{% endblock %} {% block events %}

If adventurers hang around long enough on ground floor following events take place.

  • Falok the stock merchant approaches rich looking adventurer.
  • He makes an offer to sell the adventurers Global Convoy stock.
  • This investment will make you money without working yourself!
  • Just let the Faerun Trade Group do all the lifting.
  • If they want to invest seriously he is prepared to give more information.

If adventurers come up with a serious investment a contract gets created in which the adventurers promise non-disclosure. The contract also binds the Faerun Trade Group to share the profits made through the Global Convoy project and the adventurers to pay a sum in gold.

{% endblock %} {% block locations %}

The traders guild consists of two floors. The ground floor is one big room where people seem to make agreements and exchange papers constantly. The first floor is closed to the public.

On the first floor are offices. They contains administration about guild members. And also hold copies of many big contracts made between merchants as a security measure against fire.
