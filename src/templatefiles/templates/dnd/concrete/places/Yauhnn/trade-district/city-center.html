There are also loads and loads of small places to eat and drink in this area. Some of them have great views over the sea.

Main places of interest:

  • The best fish restaurant in town. (owned by Otalan)
  • A master brewery (also owned by Otalan)
  • The Rolling Coin

There is one inn that is quite famous called The Rolling Coin It is the place for people from outside the city to stay, because their mounts will be taken care of and the good night life of the city. Some of the best bards in the world play here. If they frisk Mirika they find the keys to a room in this inn. Her stuff was stored by the inn owner.

From here adventurers can:

  • Go to big market
  • Go up the ramp to South side nobles
  • Go south to food market
