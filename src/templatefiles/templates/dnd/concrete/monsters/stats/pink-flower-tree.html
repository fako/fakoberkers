Poison

The poison of the Pink Flower Tree spreads through the water and the waterfall water vapour. Each round make a Fort save or hold your breath while in 40ft spread of the tree.

FortDC: (Pink Flower Tree poison)
20 < Initially fatigued, secondary -5 Will saves 2h, then -2 for 1h.

Flowers

One medium can lie down on the soft inner part of each flower of the tree. With psyionics the tree will attempt to make you sleep in its flowers. These flowers will perform a coup the grace with the spikes once you do.
