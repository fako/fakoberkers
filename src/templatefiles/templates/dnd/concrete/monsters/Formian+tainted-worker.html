Workers are mostly scared. Their eyes are blood red and they have wings which makes them stand out with normal Formians.

All Formians on the island have been corrupted and deprived by the evil that Hanuman's pure good soul attracts. The parasitic Stirges are the most common form of evil on the island. Therefor everything on the island takes on traits of Stirges, like red eyes, sucking blood and they have grown wings. This includes the Formians to a big degree. The queen is dead so they do not have a hive mind any more. Instead it is an everybody-for-themselves, with gruesome results. Next to this they are so close to the power of Hanuman's soul that part of its power tries to manifest in them. They gain a few of the destructive powers of Hanuman's soul.
