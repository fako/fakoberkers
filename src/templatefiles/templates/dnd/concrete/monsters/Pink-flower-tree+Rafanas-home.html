The Pink Flower Tree typically grows under waterfalls in tropical forest. The purple roots of this tree tend to curl around the rocks. From the roots on top of the cold wet rocks grow big beautiful pink flowers, that give the tree its name. These flowers look like amazing places to sit or lie down on for a while. The flower leaves are soft as cousins and smooth as silk. However the flowers are a trap from this carnivore plant.

According to legends a virgin of the pink tree is a human girl that goes to the waterfall even though her parents have forbidden her to do so. The girl will not be able to resist the poisons of the tree and falls asleep inside one of the flowers. However as the leaves of the flower are sensing out the prey of the tree, the tree may discover beauty, innocence and playfullness inside its victim. Upon this knowledge the tree may decide not to kill the girl. When the girl wakes up she will feel better than ever and as she returns to the flowers often during her childhood. The long rests, exercise in the forest, and a beautiful environment make the human girl ´blossom´. She turns into a fey with mental abilities, for as long as she remains virgin. Her mental abilities enable her to take men to her waterfall and feed the tree.
