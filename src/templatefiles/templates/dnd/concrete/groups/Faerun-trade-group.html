This group of merchants is forming an alliance to make more profit in the name of Waukeen. Their biggest project is the Global Convoy Project. This project consists of cutting transport costs/risks and evading taxes by teleporting past customs. The heresy lies in that this group took loans as if they already made the profit. Now the goods are stocked as long as the device is not ready. Trade in this stock is currently blooming.

The members of the council that form the FTG:

  • Valof (head of Waukeen temple)
  • Taduan (the Hulorn and cattle merchant)
  • Olivet
  • Dragbar (steel and weapons merchant)
