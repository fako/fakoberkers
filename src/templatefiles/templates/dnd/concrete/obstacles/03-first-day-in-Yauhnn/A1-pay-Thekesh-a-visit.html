From the outside

Thekesh lives in the cave slums. There is a terrible smell of rotting fish around his house. It is pitch black around these caves.

From the inside (entrance)

Upon entering the small 3x4 room you only see a small bed and a huge closet against the left wall.

From the inside (storage)

The house is completely piled with boxes. Inside the boxes are only fish. A lot of them very rotten. His shack is connected with a few of his neighbours through holes in the wall.

Towards the store

One of the houses has a hole in the roof that leads to a big wooden closet with a key hole. Looking through the keyhole they see a fish shop (perhaps with Moltabar)
