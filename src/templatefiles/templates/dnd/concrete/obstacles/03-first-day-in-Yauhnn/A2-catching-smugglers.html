{% extends "dnd/abstract/obstacle.html" %} {% block title %}Catching smugglers{% endblock %} {% block summary %}Either through the Red Wave or through following Thekesh/Mortabar they can discover the smuggling route{% endblock %} {% block events %}

When following Thekesh. The adventurers notice that he goes into the breakers slums at about one. They have a short briefing inside and then go to the Red Wave. Then some half-locathah and humans start to carry crates from Thekesh his place to the breakers slums. The crates contains fish Very late at night a bunch of half-locathah and humans start to carry crates towards Thekesh his place in the caves. (SearchDC will reveal mithral)

At the Red Wave when on stake out people with darkvision can roll a SpotDC20 for seeing multiple figures swimming in the water around the ship. Not long after they hear a splash and the figures swim in the direction of the breakers slums.
