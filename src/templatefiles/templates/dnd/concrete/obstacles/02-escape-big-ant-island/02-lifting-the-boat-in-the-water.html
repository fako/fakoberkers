As soon as the party gets the boat out of the jungle on the beach a Myrmarch teleports in with 4 workers. There are four scenarios:

    Hosan is helping = Fihni teleports in from a distance. Accuses Hosan of treason. He proves he's not a traitor by attacking as well. Hosan is not helping = He teleports in saying he will have to prove to Fihni he was not helping you. Hosan is dead, party killed/stole = Fihni was searching for killers/thiefs and found them Hosan is dead, party didn't do anything = Fihni teleports in asking questions, leaves

Some facts to consider during combat.

  • Beach is around 30m wide.
  • Boat weighs 400lb.
  • It takes 6 move actions to get to the sea
  • Two guys needed to move because its a clumsy object
