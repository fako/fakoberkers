As it grows darker adventurers suddenly see a slowly flickering light in the distance. A lighthouse! Meaning a harbor! (LocalDC & ReligionDC)

They may decide to enter the lighthouse/temple. Which will lead to temple of Talos. Or Pomin/Locathah may lead them there upon seeing their condition. Any contact with guards in their state will lead to prison and if they do not escape death sentence by Waukeen priests.
