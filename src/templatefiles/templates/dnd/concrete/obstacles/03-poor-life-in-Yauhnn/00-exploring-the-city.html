After receiving healing from the taint on the Big Ant Island everybody got appointed a little shack to sleep in. All shacks are part of a cave network in the cliff side. They have a beautiful view upon the sea. You were given very simple cloths (1 sp) that smell like the slums. A mixture of rotting fish and dried sweat

Before letting you rest Janala lets you know that the entire slums are there to help. As an Oracle of Talos she also offers any kind of service that you may need (dirty -> WillDC). You can find her in the Temple of Talos. She hopes to speak tomorrow when you are more familiar with the city and she has done her prayers.

When paying something in the city there is a 60% change that they get back Waukeen coins (illusionary gold).
