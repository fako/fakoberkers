As you walk towards the church you are startled by shouting behind you. Two rough sailors are having a loud argument about money in front of a pity street merchant. They draw a lot of attention and start a fist fight (roll initiative).

Round by round:

  1. A SpotDC20 will make adventurers notice somebody walking quick towards the church as soon as the first punch hits. SenseMotiveDC10 indicates he is up to something.
  2. The person heads towards the elevator and becomes invisible there.
  3. The person takes 1 rounds to climb the ropes of the elevator
  4. Kalong get attacked while praying
  5. Fight lasts until pirate or inquisitor is dead. Barbarians break up the fight.
