Those who have died are in heaven/hell for one year It is amazing/awful. After one year you are called towards the living. To come back to life you need a spark, some inspiration to get back into a physical body again. You need to choose what that spark is. Is it to drink, to eat, to sleep, to breath or to have sex at least one more time?

Your last memory is that you went to sleep in a warm bed. Now you wake up completely naked in a strong but warm wind. You lie in fine yellow sand and can hear waves pounding hard on the shore. As you turn your head you see your comrades lying not far from you.
